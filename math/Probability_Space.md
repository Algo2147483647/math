## Define

$$
<立, \mathcal F, \mathbb P>  \tag{Probability Space}
$$
Probability Space is a [measure space](./Measurable_Space.md) including, 

- $立$: sample space
- $\mathbb P$: Probability, a measure of the sample space $立$.
- $\mathcal F$: a $\sigma$-algebra of the sample space $立$.
